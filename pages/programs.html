Our Key Programs

Comprehensive programs addressing the diverse needs of persons with albinism

Sunscreen Distribution Program

Essential sun protection for daily life

Our flagship program provides free sunscreen and protective clothing to persons with albinism across Zambia, ensuring daily protection from harmful UV radiation.

  • Monthly sunscreen distribution
  • Protective clothing provision
  • Wide-brimmed hats and umbrellas
  • UV protective eyewear
  • Home delivery for remote areas
Monthly Beneficiaries 350+

Educational Support & Scholarships

Breaking barriers to quality education

Comprehensive educational support ensuring persons with albinism have equal access to quality education from primary school through university level.

  • School fees assistance
  • Learning materials and supplies
  • Low vision aids and magnifiers
  • University scholarships
  • Inclusive education advocacy
Students Supported 120+

Healthcare Access Initiative

Specialized medical care and support

Facilitating access to specialized healthcare services, skin cancer screening, and vision care for persons with albinism.

  • Regular skin examinations
  • Vision screening and eyecare
  • Dermatology consultations
  • Medical referrals and support
  • Health education workshops
Medical Consultations 200+/year

Community Empowerment & Skills Training

Building self-reliance and economic independence

Skills development and economic empowerment programs designed to create sustainable livelihoods and financial independence for persons with albinism.

  • Vocational skills training
  • Business development support
  • Microfinance and loans
  • Entrepreneurship workshops
  • Job placement assistance
Participants Trained 85+

Awareness & Advocacy Campaigns

Changing attitudes and building inclusive communities

Comprehensive awareness campaigns and advocacy initiatives aimed at combating discrimination and promoting understanding of albinism in communities.

  • Community sensitization programs
  • School awareness visits
  • Media campaigns and outreach
  • Policy advocacy initiatives
  • Cultural leader engagement
People Reached 10,000+

Support Groups & Peer Networks

Building community and mutual support

Peer support networks and support groups that provide emotional support, guidance, and community connection for persons with albinism and their families.

  • Monthly support group meetings
  • Peer mentorship programs
  • Family support services
  • Psychological counseling
  • Social events and gatherings
Active Members 150+
