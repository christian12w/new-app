Policy Achievements

2024

Education Inclusion Guidelines

Successfully advocated for the adoption of comprehensive education inclusion guidelines that mandate schools to provide necessary accommodations for students with albinism, including preferential seating and visual aids.

2023

Healthcare Access Expansion

Collaborated with the Ministry of Health to expand specialized healthcare services for persons with albinism, including the establishment of dedicated clinics in major provinces.

2022

Anti-Discrimination Framework

Contributed to the development of a national anti-discrimination framework that specifically addresses discrimination against persons with albinism in employment and public services.

2021

Sunscreen Subsidy Program

Advocated for and helped establish a government subsidy program that provides free or subsidized sunscreen and protective clothing to persons with albinism across Zambia.

Key Achievements

Policy Changes

Successfully influenced 12 major policy changes at national and provincial levels, improving access to education, healthcare, and employment opportunities.

Awareness Campaigns

Conducted over 50 awareness campaigns reaching more than 100,000 people, significantly reducing stigma and misconceptions about albinism.

Legal Victories

Won 8 landmark legal cases that established important precedents for protecting the rights of persons with albinism in Zambia.

Strategic Partnerships

Built partnerships with 25+ organizations, government agencies, and international bodies to amplify our advocacy efforts and impact.
